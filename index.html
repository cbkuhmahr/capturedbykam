Terms & Session Details

Booking & Payment
A 50% non-refundable retainer is required to book and hold your session date. The remaining balance is due on or before the day of the session.

Turnaround
Final edited images are delivered within 7–14 days. Rush delivery may be available.

Creative Direction
You’re booking my eye, my process, and my edit style. I guide posing, framing, and final image selection to deliver work that reflects the Captured by Kam look—timeless, intentional, and authentic.

Image Delivery
Final edited images are delivered digitally via an online gallery. RAW / unedited files are not provided.

Usage
Images are delivered for personal use unless a commercial agreement is arranged in advance.

Rescheduling
Weather and life happen. When possible, sessions may be rescheduled to a new date.
